Scope

Primary focus: seasonal influenza vaccines and flu-containing combination vaccines in active development, regulatory review, or early authorization.

Priority geographies: Canada, United States, European Union, United Kingdom, Australia, and similar high-income seasonal vaccine markets.

Important: Seed records are manually curated starters, not an exhaustive systematic review.

How this dashboard should be maintained

Verification labels

seed-manual records were manually entered with a source URL. auto-unverified records are harvested by scripts and must be checked before synthesis. curator-gap entries are evidence-map priorities rather than external evidence.

Review cadence

Run automated source checks weekly. Manually review regulatory agency pages before influenza vaccine advisory committee meetings, WHO/FDA/EMA strain-composition updates, and sponsor quarterly results.

Inclusion rule

Include records if they concern seasonal influenza vaccine efficacy, immunogenicity, safety, regulatory status, policy, access, or correlates; prioritize candidates plausibly relevant to Canada, the US, Europe, or comparable markets.
